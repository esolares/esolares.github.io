## Course Overview

**CSE 150B: Introduction to AI - Search and Reasoning** is an undergraduate course in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at UC San Diego.

## Topics Covered

- Search algorithms (BFS, DFS, A*, etc.)
- Game playing and adversarial search
- Constraint satisfaction problems
- Logical reasoning and inference
- Knowledge representation
- Planning algorithms
